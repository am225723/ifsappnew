# Meditation Recording Scripts

These are the narration scripts for the 11 remaining guided meditations. One meditation — **Connect with Self Energy** — has already been recorded (`cultivating-self-energy.mp3`).

After recording, place each MP3 file in `public/audio/meditations/` using the suggested filename.

---

## 1. Parts Check-In
**Category:** Parts Work | **Duration:** 4 minutes | **Filename:** `parts-check-in.mp3`

> Settle into stillness. Take three slow breaths. With each exhale, release any tension you're holding.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Turn your attention inward. Gently ask: "Who's here right now? Which parts of me want to be noticed?"
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Wait patiently. A part might show up as a feeling, an image, a thought, or a sensation in your body.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> When you notice a part, greet it with warmth. Ask: "What do you want me to know?" Listen without judgment.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Thank this part for showing up. Ask: "What do you need from me right now?" Simply listen.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Send this part compassion and understanding. Let it know that you, the Self, are here and paying attention.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Check if any other parts want to be acknowledged. If so, repeat the process of greeting and listening.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Thank all parts that showed up. Take a final deep breath and slowly return to the present moment.

## 2. Create an Inner Safe Place
**Category:** Grounding | **Duration:** 6 minutes | **Filename:** `inner-safe-place.mp3`

> Close your eyes and take five deep, slow breaths. With each breath, let yourself sink deeper into relaxation.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Imagine yourself walking along a peaceful path. The air is warm and gentle. You feel safe.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Ahead, you see a place that feels completely safe — it could be a meadow, a beach, a cozy room, or anywhere that feels right.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Step into this place. Look around. What do you see? Notice the colors, the light, the textures.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> What do you hear? Maybe birds singing, water flowing, or peaceful silence. Let the sounds comfort you.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> What do you feel? The ground beneath you, the temperature of the air, a gentle breeze perhaps.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Find a comfortable spot to rest. Sit or lie down. This is your sacred space. No one can enter without your permission.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> In this safe place, you are free from worry, free from pain, free from the demands of the world.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> If any part of you is hurting, you can invite it here. Show it this safe place. Tell it: "You can rest here."
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Spend a moment just being here. Absorb the peace. Know that this place lives inside you always.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Slowly begin to return. Bring the feeling of safety with you. Wiggle your fingers, take a deep breath, and open your eyes.

## 3. Appreciate Your Protectors
**Category:** Parts Work | **Duration:** 5 minutes | **Filename:** `protector-appreciation.mp3`

> Get comfortable and take several calming breaths. Let yourself settle into a quiet, receptive state.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Think about a protector part — a part that works hard to keep you safe, even if it sometimes causes problems.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Notice where this protector lives in your body. It might feel like tension, tightness, or energy somewhere.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Instead of wanting it to change, try something different. Say: "I see how hard you work. Thank you for protecting me."
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Notice what happens. The protector might soften, or it might be surprised. Just stay present with it.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Ask: "What are you afraid would happen if you stopped protecting me?" Listen with genuine curiosity.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Whatever it shares, respond with understanding: "That makes sense. I understand why you work so hard."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Ask: "Is there anything you need from me?" Let the protector know that you, the Self, are growing stronger.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Send this protector gratitude and compassion. It has been doing its best with what it knows.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Take a deep breath. Carry this appreciation with you as you gently return to the present.

## 4. IFS Body Scan
**Category:** Grounding | **Duration:** 6 minutes | **Filename:** `ifs-body-scan.mp3`

> Lie down or sit comfortably. Close your eyes. Take three deep breaths to center yourself.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Bring your attention to the top of your head. Notice any sensations — tingling, warmth, pressure, or nothing at all.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Move your awareness down to your face. Notice your forehead, eyes, jaw. Are any parts holding tension here?
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Scan through your neck and shoulders. These areas often hold protector energy. Notice without trying to change anything.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Move into your chest and heart area. This is where many people feel their exile's pain or their Self energy. What do you notice?
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> If you notice a strong sensation, pause here. Ask: "Is this a part? What does it want me to know?"
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Continue down to your belly. The gut often holds intuition and younger parts. Breathe into this space.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Scan through your hips, legs, and feet. Notice any areas of holding, numbness, or warmth.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Now expand your awareness to your whole body at once. You are a living system of parts, all working together.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Send appreciation to your entire body and all the parts living within it. They are all welcome here.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Take three more deep breaths. Slowly open your eyes and return to the room, carrying awareness with you.

## 5. Compassion Breathing
**Category:** Foundation | **Duration:** 3 minutes | **Filename:** `compassion-breathing.mp3`

> Sit quietly. Place one hand on your heart and one on your belly. Feel the warmth of your own touch.
>
> *(pause 20 seconds)*
>
> Breathe in slowly for 4 counts. Hold for 4 counts. Breathe out for 6 counts. Repeat this pattern.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> As you breathe in, imagine inhaling golden compassion. As you exhale, release any judgment or criticism.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> With each breath, silently repeat: "I am worthy of compassion. I am worthy of love. I am enough."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> If a critical part speaks up, acknowledge it gently: "I hear you. Right now, we're practicing compassion."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Feel the warmth under your hands. Your body is responding to your own kindness. This is Self energy.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Take one final deep breath of compassion. Carry this warmth with you into the rest of your day.

## 6. Healing Your Exile
**Category:** Parts Work | **Duration:** 6 minutes | **Filename:** `exile-healing.mp3`

> Find a quiet, comfortable space. Close your eyes and take several slow breaths. Let your body settle and your mind grow still.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> From your Self — that calm, wise center within you — gently turn your attention inward. You are looking for a younger part, an exile, who carries old pain.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> You may sense this part as a child version of yourself, or as a feeling, an image, or a memory. Let it come to you naturally.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Notice where this part lives in your body. Perhaps your chest, your stomach, or your throat. Breathe warmth toward that area.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Let this young part know you see it. Say gently: "I'm here now. I see you. You are not alone anymore."
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Ask this part: "What happened to you? What do you want me to know?" Listen with your heart, not your head.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> Whatever this part shares — sadness, fear, loneliness — receive it with compassion. You don't need to fix it. Just witness it.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Ask: "What do you need from me right now?" It might need comfort, safety, to be held, or simply to be believed.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Offer this part exactly what it needs. Imagine yourself holding, comforting, or protecting this young one.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Let this part know: "You don't have to carry this alone anymore. I am here. I am strong enough now."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Take a few deep breaths. Thank this exile for trusting you. Slowly return your awareness to the room.

## 7. Grounding Anchor
**Category:** Grounding | **Duration:** 3 minutes | **Filename:** `grounding-anchor.mp3`

> Press your feet firmly into the floor. Feel the solid ground beneath you. You are here. You are safe. You are present.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Name five things you can see. Look around slowly. Notice colors, shapes, light. Let each observation anchor you here.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Name four things you can touch. Feel the texture of your clothing, the chair beneath you, the air on your skin.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Name three things you can hear. Perhaps distant sounds, your own breathing, the hum of the room.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Name two things you can smell. Breathe deeply. Notice whatever scents are present, even subtle ones.
>
> *(pause 20 seconds)*
>
> Name one thing you can taste. Notice the taste in your mouth right now, whatever it may be.
>
> *(pause 20 seconds)*
>
> Place both hands on your chest. Feel your heartbeat. This rhythm has been with you since before you were born. It is your anchor.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Take three final breaths. You are grounded. You are present. You are safe. Carry this groundedness with you.

## 8. Inner Child Reconnection
**Category:** Parts Work | **Duration:** 5 minutes | **Filename:** `inner-child-reconnection.mp3`

> Close your eyes and smile gently. Today, you are going to reconnect with the playful, joyful part of your younger self.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Imagine yourself walking into a beautiful garden. The sun is warm, flowers are blooming, and you feel light and free.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> In this garden, you see a child — your inner child. Notice how old they are, what they look like, what they're doing.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Walk toward them slowly. Kneel down so you're at their eye level. Let them see your face — kind, warm, loving.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Ask your inner child: "What would you like to do together?" Maybe they want to play, draw, run, dance, or just sit together.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Join them in whatever they choose. Let yourself feel the joy of play, the freedom of being a child with no responsibilities.
>
> *(pause 40 seconds)*
>
> As you play together, tell your inner child: "I love spending time with you. You matter to me. Your joy matters."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Ask: "What else do you need from me?" Listen to what comes up — perhaps more play, more attention, more laughter in daily life.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Give your inner child a warm hug. Promise to visit this garden again soon. This connection is always available to you.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Slowly bring your awareness back to the present. Carry the warmth of this connection into your day.

## 9. Tension Release & Letting Go
**Category:** Grounding | **Duration:** 4 minutes | **Filename:** `tension-release.mp3`

> Sit or lie down comfortably. Take a deep breath and let out a long, slow sigh. Give yourself permission to release.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Squeeze your fists tightly for five seconds. Feel the tension build. Now release. Notice the relief as your hands soften.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Lift your shoulders up to your ears. Hold the tension. Now drop them. Feel the weight fall away.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Clench your jaw tightly. Hold for a moment. Now let it go. Let your mouth fall slightly open. Feel the release.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Tighten your whole face — scrunch everything up. Hold. Now release completely. Feel your face smooth and soft.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Tense your whole body — every muscle. Hold for five seconds. And release everything at once. Feel the wave of relaxation.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Now scan your body for any remaining tension. Wherever you find it, breathe into that area and imagine it melting away.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Ask yourself: "What emotional weight am I carrying today?" Name it silently. Then imagine setting it down beside you.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Take three cleansing breaths. You have released what you can. What remains, you can carry more lightly. Open your eyes gently.

## 10. Self-Forgiveness Practice
**Category:** Foundation | **Duration:** 5 minutes | **Filename:** `self-forgiveness.mp3`

> Settle into a comfortable position. Close your eyes. Place a hand over your heart and feel its steady rhythm.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Think of something you've been holding against yourself — a mistake, a regret, a way you feel you've fallen short.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Notice the part of you that carries guilt or shame about this. Where does it live in your body? Acknowledge it gently.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Recognize that this part has been trying to make you better by holding onto this pain. Thank it for its intention.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Now, from your Self, speak to this part: "You were doing the best you could with what you knew at the time."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Repeat silently: "I forgive myself for being human. I forgive myself for not knowing then what I know now."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Imagine the guilt or shame as a heavy stone you've been carrying. Set it down. You don't need it to grow.
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Feel the lightness as you release this weight. Breathe into the space that opens up. Fill it with compassion.
>
> *(pause 35 seconds)*
>
> Tell yourself: "I am worthy of forgiveness. I am learning. I am growing. That is enough."
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Take three deep breaths of self-compassion. Open your eyes when ready, carrying this forgiveness forward.

## 11. Morning Intention Setting
**Category:** Foundation | **Duration:** 3 minutes | **Filename:** `morning-intention.mp3`

> Good morning. Before the day carries you away, take this moment to arrive fully. Three deep breaths.
>
> *(pause 20 seconds)*
>
> Check in with your system. How are you feeling right now? What parts are already active this morning?
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Acknowledge whatever is present without judgment. "I notice I'm feeling..." Just observe.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Now, set an intention for today. Not a task — an intention. How do you want to show up? What quality do you want to lead with?
>
> *(pause 30 seconds)*
>
> Perhaps today your intention is patience, or courage, or presence, or gentleness with yourself.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Speak your intention silently: "Today, I choose to lead with..." Let it settle into your body.
>
> *(pause 25 seconds)*
>
> Take one final breath. Carry your intention like a compass guiding you through whatever this day brings. You are ready.
