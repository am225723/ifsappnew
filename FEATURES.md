# IFS Program - Complete Feature List

## 🎯 Main Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Beautiful gradient design with app introduction
- **Feature Cards**: 8 interactive cards linking to main sections
- **Philosophy Section**: "No Bad Parts" core principle explanation
- **Key Concepts**: Overview of Self, Protectors, and Exiles
- **Call-to-Action**: Prominent "Begin Your Journey" button

### 2. IFS Cheat Sheet
- **Overview Section**: Complete IFS model explanation
- **Parts Categories**: 
  - Self (core identity)
  - Managers (protective parts)
  - Firefighters (reactive parts)
  - Exiles (vulnerable parts)
- **Therapeutic Techniques**: 4 key techniques with descriptions
- **Therapy Goals**: 4 main objectives
- **Treatment Approaches**: Direct Access, Witnessing, Unburdening
- **6 F's Approach**: Interactive display of all 6 steps

### 3. 10 Common Wounds
- **Expandable Cards**: Click to reveal detailed information
- **Color-Coded**: Each wound has unique gradient color
- **Three Sections per Wound**:
  - Root Cause
  - Child Manifestations
  - Adult Manifestations
- **Healing Message**: Encouraging conclusion section
- **Wounds Covered**:
  1. Abandonment
  2. Neglect
  3. Rejection
  4. Criticism/Shame
  5. Betrayal
  6. Humiliation
  7. Injustice
  8. Loss/Grief
  9. Emotional Invalidation
  10. Trauma

### 4. Qualities of Self
- **Tabbed Interface**: Switch between 8 C's and 5 P's
- **8 C's of Self**:
  - Calmness
  - Curiosity
  - Clarity
  - Compassion
  - Confidence
  - Courage
  - Creativity
  - Connectedness
- **5 P's of Self**:
  - Patience
  - Persistence
  - Presence
  - Playfulness
  - Peace
- **Integration Section**: Practical guidance for embodying qualities
- **Beautiful Cards**: Gradient backgrounds for each quality

### 5. Parts Mapping
- **Four Main Sections**:
  
  **a) Overview**
  - Parts categories explanation
  - 5-step mapping process
  - Visual diagrams
  
  **b) 6 F's Approach**
  - Find
  - Focus
  - Flesh Out
  - Feel Toward
  - Befriend
  - Fears
  
  **c) Unburdening Process**
  - 5 detailed steps
  - Important notes and guidelines
  
  **d) My Parts Map**
  - Create new parts
  - Edit existing parts
  - Delete parts
  - Track:
    * Part name
    * Category (Manager/Firefighter/Exile)
    * Description
    * Triggers
    * Protective intention
  - Color-coded by category
  - Full CRUD functionality

### 6. Guided Exercises
- **Three Complete Exercises**:
  
  **a) Meet Your Parts (10 minutes)**
  - 7 guided steps
  - Gentle introduction to parts work
  
  **b) Self-Energy Check-In (5 minutes)**
  - 7 steps to assess Self-connection
  - 8 C's evaluation
  
  **c) Dialogue with a Part (15 minutes)**
  - 8 steps for deep part communication
  - Compassionate conversation guide

- **Features**:
  - Step-by-step navigation
  - Progress bar
  - Suggested durations
  - Previous/Next/Reset buttons
  - All steps preview
  - Click any step to jump to it

### 7. Self-Assessment
- **Three Comprehensive Quizzes**:
  
  **a) Identify Your Core Wounds**
  - 10 questions
  - Maps to 10 common wounds
  
  **b) Identify Your Protective Parts**
  - 10 questions
  - Distinguishes Managers vs Firefighters
  
  **c) Self-Energy Assessment**
  - 8 questions
  - Evaluates 8 C's connection

- **Features**:
  - 5-point Likert scale
  - Progress tracking
  - Visual results with scoring
  - Top 3 areas identified
  - Color-coded results
  - Interpretation guidance
  - Review answers option
  - Reset functionality

### 8. Resource Library
- **10 Curated Resources**:
  - Books (IFS foundational texts)
  - Videos (training and courses)
  - Audio (podcasts and meditations)
  
- **Features**:
  - Search functionality
  - Category filtering (All, Foundational, Practical, Trauma, Training)
  - Type icons (Book, Video, Audio)
  - External links
  - Official IFS resources section
  - Therapist finder links
  - Downloadable resources section

### 9. Personal Journal
- **Full Journaling System**:
  - Create entries with:
    * Title
    * Date
    * Mood (6 options with emojis)
    * Parts involved
    * Content (rich text)
  - View all entries
  - Delete entries
  - Export journal (JSON)
  - Import journal (JSON)
  - Local storage persistence

- **Journal Prompts**:
  - 6 reflective prompts to guide writing
  
- **Mood Options**:
  - 😊 Happy
  - 😌 Calm
  - 😐 Neutral
  - 😰 Anxious
  - 😢 Sad
  - 😠 Angry
